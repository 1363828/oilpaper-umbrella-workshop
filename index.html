About oilpaper umbrella

Oilpaper umbrella has abundant traditional cultural connotations. As we all know, it is a symbol of love. Many love stories have taken place under oilpaper umbrellas throughout the ages. Each part of the oilpaper umbrella has a beautiful meaning. The umbrella ribs are made of bamboo which means getting promoted swiftly. The shape of the umbrella is round, which symbolizes happiness, reunion and safeness. In addition, oilpaper umbrellas have a long history and have been inherited for thousands of years.，which show the crystallization of the wisdom of Chinese people.
Characteristics: Teamwork, traditional culture, meaningful, enjoyable, fruitful
Language: both Chinese and English

Activity process

Traditional oilpaper umbrella making procedure is very complex and also time consuming for the whole process is completely by hand. However, in our activity, you can experience almost every part of the procedure in a simpler way. The processes are as follows:
1) We first introduce ourselves and I would introduce the history and cultural meanings of oiled umbrella.
2) You are supposed to work with 1 or 2 people in a group to stick the cotton paper to the umbrella frame. You are advised to cooperate to cut the cotton paper to a particular shape according with the ready-made umbrella frame. And then, you should make the cotton paper adhere to the frame using the glue provided.
3) Then you could draw on the blank oilpaper umbrella. As the most interesting part of this activity, draw whatever you want on the umbrella fabric to beautify your oilpaper umbrella by using your imagination and creativity.
4) After painting on the umbrella’s fabric, you need to make the umbrella water-proof. To achieve this, you need to brush Tong oil over the umbrella fabric, which is also considered to be a protection of the paintings.
5) To let the oil just brushed dry, the umbrellas need to take a few-days sunbathing, which is the very last step of this D.I.Y activity. After exposure to sunlight, finally you will get the perfect traditional oiled paper umbrella.
